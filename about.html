How We Started

Born from a
simple desire.

Treetop Business Solutions was born from a simple desire — to help people succeed at what they do best. After seeing several incredibly talented friends pour their hearts into starting businesses, only to watch them close because they didn't have support in areas like financial record keeping, we knew there was a gap we could fill.

We realized that many small business owners were trying to do it all, and it was costing them their dreams. That's when we decided to step in and do what we love: help others grow by handling the behind-the-scenes work that can make or break a business.

Treetop is more than a business to us — it's a passion project rooted in the belief that with the right support, every great idea has a chance to thrive.

"Everyone at Treetop has a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Our desire is to glorify Him in all we do."
